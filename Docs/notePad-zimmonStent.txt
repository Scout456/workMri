
Zimmon Biliary Stent is made of plastic. Biliary stents are tubular devices that are used to treat or prevent blockages in the bile duct. They can be made from a variety of materials, including:

●Polyethylene
●Polyurethane
●Teflon
●Soft polymer blend
●A combination of layers, such as a stainless steel middle layer and a polyamide elastomer outer layer
