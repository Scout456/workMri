Kyleena, an intrauterine device (IUD), can be safely scanned with magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) under specific conditions. These conditions include:

* A static magnetic field of 3.0 T or less
* A maximum spatial field gradient of 36,000 gauss/cm (360 T/m)
* A whole body averaged specific absorption rate (SAR) of 4 W/kg (First Level Controlled Operating Mode)
