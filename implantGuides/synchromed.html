Medtronic SynchroMed Infusion Systems

Applicable Models

SynchroMed 3, Model #8667

Scanner Parameters

Philips Ingenia 1.5T

The referring physician or consulting physician must confirm, in writing, that the patient may safely be deprived of drug delivery. The radiologist must confirm that the pump is not oriented 90° to the z-axis. Move the patient into and out of Zone IV slowly to minimize Lenz’s forces. Scan only in Normal Operating Mode. Do not exceed 30 minutes of continuous scan time per 90-minute window. Maintain communication with the patient at all times. Assess for heating, excessive vibration, or other disturbances. Verify that the patient is feeling normal and is responsive between each sequence. After the scan, the clinician or Medtronic must interrogate the pump to ensure proper function
